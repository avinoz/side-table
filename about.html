    About

    We're locals too!

    Side Table is a neighborhood coffeehouse coming to South Berkeley—built around quality drinks, thoughtful food, and room to linger. More on our story soon.

    Coffee, Tea, & Great Company

    Welcome to Side Table — your local hangout for a proper caffeine fix, a great lunch, and a serious slice of cake. We’re building a bright, open space with plenty of room to spread out, whether you’re catching up with friends over a long lunch or finally tackling that book you’ve been meaning to finish.

    More Than Just a Quick Stop

    We’re here for more than just your morning rush. Our menu is built on fresh, simple ingredients that hit the spot:

    The Counter:

    Freshly baked cakes and pastries that change with the seasons.

    The Kitchen:

    Hearty sandwiches and vibrant, seasonal salads made to order.

    The Bar:

    Expertly pulled espresso and a massive library of loose-leaf teas.

    Make Yourself at Home

    With plenty of seating and a laid-back vibe, we’re the neighborhood’s go-to spot to actually sit down and stay a while. It’s a big, welcoming space designed for slow weekend brunches, mid-day meetings, or just escaping the house for a bit of atmosphere.

    Take the Vibe Home

    Love what we’re brewing? Grab a bag of our signature beans or a tin of our house tea on your way out. Planning a get-together? Ask us about our whole cakes and catering platters for your next party.

    Come Hang Out

    Open daily from the early morning spark to the afternoon slump. Pull up a chair—there’s always a spot for you at the table.
