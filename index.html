    About Us

    By locals.
    For locals

    At Side Table, we believe neighborhoods are built on shared stories. We’ve traded the "to-go" hustle for a spacious South Berkeley sanctuary designed for you to settle in. Whether you're here for a slow brunch or a change of scenery from your home office, our quality coffee and seasonal menu are all about helping you hit pause and feel at home.

    What We're Bringing

    Single Origin Brews

    Expertly pulled espresso and a curated library of loose-leaf teas. We source direct from the farms, single origin beans and tea leaves.

    Come Hang Out

    We’re the neighborhood’s go-to spot to actually sit down and stay a while. It’s a big, welcoming space designed for slow weekend brunches, mid-day meetings, or just escaping the house for a bit of atmosphere.

    Artisanal food

    Locally sourced fresh cakes and pastries that change with the seasons along with hearty sandwiches and vibrant, salads from fresh ingredients. Stay local and fresh.
