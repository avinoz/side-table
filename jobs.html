    Careers

    Join our team

    We’re building a neighborhood coffeehouse in South Berkeley. If you’d like to work with us, send a note—we’ll reach out as we staff up for opening.

    We're looking for baristas & a manager

    Compensation & benefits

    We intend to offer a competitive hourly rate plus tips, with details finalized closer to hire. We’re committed to predictable scheduling, paid sick time in line with California law, and shift perks including drinks, meals where applicable, and team discounts.

    Schedule

    Side Table is slated to open summer 2026. We expect a mix of full-time and part-time shifts covering mornings through late nights—exact hours will mirror our published shop hours once we’re live. Weekend availability is a strong plus for cafe roles.

    What you’ll gain

    • Hands-on training on espresso, tea service, and assemble food in our kitchen.
    • A collaborative crew focused on hospitality and consistency—not rush alone.
    • Be part of our communtiy in South Berkeley.

    Equal opportunity

    Side Table is an equal opportunity employer. We welcome applicants of all backgrounds and do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability, age, or veteran status.

    Our Values

    Side Table is a neighborhood coffee and tea house—great drinks, real food, and room to stay awhile. We care about the human connection in our community above all else.

    How to apply

    Email hello@sidetableberkeley.com with the subject line Jobs. Include a short note about yourself, the role you’re interested in, and your general availability. A resume or work history is welcome but not required to start the conversation.
